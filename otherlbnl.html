Selected Applied Science Projects

A selection of recent work spanning scientific interfaces, data publishing workflows, and domain research tools.

WELDER

WELDER enables facility managers and policy makers to model how extreme weather events might reprioritize maintenance schedules for facilities and buildings. The “Climate Recon” app visualizes how extreme weather events are projected to change over time.

ESS-DIVE

The Environmental Systems Science Data Infrastructure for a Virtual Ecosystem (ESS-DIVE) is a repository for Earth and environmental science data. The concept below explores a streamlined data-package publishing workflow.

Science Capsule

Science Capsule helps scientists capture and manage next-generation knowledge discovery workflows. Shown here as a JupyterLab extension integrated with a notebook, the app captures data-generation events alongside other workflow processes.

PARETO

PARETO enables environmentally sustainable ways to manage, treat, and beneficially reuse water produced from oil and gas operations. This concept explores key flows and UI components supporting planning and reuse.
